The Office of St. Cecilia.

Edition.

The link above constitutes a performance booklet for the offices of First Vespers, Matins, Lauds, and Second Vespers for the feast of St. Cecilia. These are the offices containing all the musical items proper to the feast. The edition contains all material necessary for a complete and faithful reproduction of each office, except Second Vespers, the performance of which must account for ferial psalms.

The offices, in an abbreviated form featuring only the propers, were orginally prepared for a special session at the 51st International Congress on Medieval Studies. There followed a presentation and performance at the 13th Annual Colloquium of the Gregorian Institute of Canada.

Second Vespers, truly First Vespers of St. Clement, is a commemoration to Cecilia wherein the five antiphons of Lauds are repeated and a memorial antiphon is sung after the close of the service.
